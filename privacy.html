Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

The Short Version

Overlap does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays in your browser.

What We Don't Collect

We don't collect anything. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies, no account creation, no email addresses. Nothing.

Local Storage

Your timezone preferences are saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it anytime by clearing your browser data.

Third-Party Services

When you click "Add to Calendar", you're redirected to Google Calendar, Microsoft Teams, or Zoom. Those services have their own privacy policies. We don't receive any data from them.

Chrome Extension

The Overlap Chrome extension works entirely offline and locally. It does not communicate with any servers or collect any data.
